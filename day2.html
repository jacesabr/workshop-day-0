# judging.md what we're looking for
  • It works. A live URL anyone can open and use. If localhost is the only place it runs, it didn't ship.
  • It solves something real. One problem your team actually has — or someone you know has.
  • It uses AI well. Wire your GEMINI_API_KEY in so the app thinks, not just stores.
  • You can demo in five minutes. Story → problem → live walkthrough → URL. The app is the slides.
