🏅 What we're looking for

  • It works. A live URL anyone can open and use. If it's not deployed, it didn't happen.
  • It solves something real. One problem your team actually has, or someone you know has.
  • It uses AI well. The Gemini key is in your .env — wire it in so the app thinks, not just stores.
  • You can demo in 5 minutes. Story, problem, walkthrough, URL. No slides needed — the app is the slides.
